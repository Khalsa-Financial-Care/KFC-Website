Our Mission
At Khalsa Financial Care, our mission is to empower new Punjabi immigrants with the financial knowledge and tools necessary to thrive in America. We envision a community where every new immigrant is equipped with the knowledge to make informed financial decisions. By providing education on essential topics such as budgeting, saving, investing, and retirement planning, we aim to bridge the knowledge gap and foster financial independence.
Workshops
We host regular workshops covering a range of financial topics, specifically tailored to meet the needs of our community. These sessions are interactive, ensuring participants gain practical knowledge they can apply immediately.
Our Approach
  • Workshops: Interactive sessions covering budgeting, investing, and more.
  • Resources: Easy-to-understand materials to support continued learning.
  • One-on-One Guidance: Personalized support to help individuals reach their financial goals.

Accomplishments
Ceremonial Resolution from the New Jersey General Assembly
Our co-founders Pahul Virk and Amrit Mann proudly received a prestigious ceremonial resolution from NJ Assemblywoman Andrea Katz on behalf of the New Jersey General Assembly, signed by the Speaker and Clerk of the House. This honor was bestowed in recognition of our outstanding work at Khalsa Financial Care, particularly our dedication to empowering immigrant communities through financial literacy education.
